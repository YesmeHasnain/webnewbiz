Meet
Coach Coop.

As a player, Coop was known for his efficient shooting, long-range shooting ability, and one-on-one moves. He finished his college career as the school’s only three-time All-American and was later inducted into the Hall of Fame.

Coop also scored 2,037 points (2nd all-time in school history) while shooting 54% from the field and 46% from the 3-point line.

After finishing his career at Wooster, Coop defied the odds as one of very few 6’0″ guards to play professionally in Dortmund, Germany. Today, he is dedicated to helping athletes take their game to the next level.
