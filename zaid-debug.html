Meet
Coach Coop.

As a player, Coop was known for his efficient shooting, long-range shooting ability, and one-on-one moves. He finished his college career as the school’s only three-time All-American and was later inducted into the Hall of Fame.

Coop scored 2,037 points (2nd all-time) while shooting 54% from the field and 46% from the 3-point line.

After Wooster, Coop defied the odds as one of very few 6’0″ guards to play professionally in Dortmund, Germany.
